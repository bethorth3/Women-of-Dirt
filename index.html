We love dirt!

The Women of Dirt is a chapter of the Fearless Women of Dirt Organization. This chapter is based in the Linn county area.

We are a group of women who love to ride dirt trails, mountain bikes, gravel, or pavement. Let's be honest we just like to ride. Mostly we ride on dirt trails in Linn County. Check out our links for clinics to help you get better, sweet new trails to ride, or places to get new wheels.
